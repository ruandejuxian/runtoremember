Our Story

Run to Remember is a nonprofit organization dedicated to connecting communities through the power of movement. We believe that every mile you cover is a profound tribute to those who are no longer with us.

All funds raised are directly donated to local healthcare initiatives and education funds in the United States, ensuring that your sweat and dedication create a lasting legacy for others.
